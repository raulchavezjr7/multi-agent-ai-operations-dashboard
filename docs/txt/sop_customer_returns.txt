Timber & Tools Co. — Customer Return Policy

Customers may return most items within 30 days with a receipt. Returns without a receipt require manager approval and may be refunded as store credit.

Power tools must be returned within 15 days and must include all accessories.
Cut lumber, mixed paint, clearance items, and special orders are non‑returnable.

During system outages, returns are temporarily suspended.

Employees must inspect all returned items for damage or missing parts before processing the refund.
