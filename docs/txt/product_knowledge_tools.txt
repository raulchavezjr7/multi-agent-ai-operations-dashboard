Timber & Tools Co. — Tools Product Knowledge

The Tools department includes hand tools, power tools, and accessories.

Cordless drills are the most popular power tool and are available in 12V, 18V, and 20V systems.
Circular saws are used for straight cuts in lumber and sheet goods.
Jigsaws are used for curved or detailed cuts.

Hand tools include hammers, screwdrivers, wrenches, pliers, and measuring tools.

Employees should help customers compare features such as battery life, torque, blade type, and warranty coverage.
