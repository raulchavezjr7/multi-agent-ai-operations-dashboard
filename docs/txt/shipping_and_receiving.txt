Timber & Tools Co. — Shipping and Receiving Procedures

Deliveries arrive between 6:00 AM and 11:00 AM.
The receiving clerk must inspect all pallets for damage before signing the bill of lading.

Items should be scanned into inventory before being moved to the sales floor.
Oversized items such as lumber bundles must be transported using a pallet jack or forklift.

All packing materials must be disposed of in the designated recycling bins.
