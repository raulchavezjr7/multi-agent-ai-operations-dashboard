Timber & Tools Co. — System Outage Procedure

If the point‑of‑sale or inventory system goes offline, employees must follow the outage procedure.

1. Notify the shift manager immediately.

2. Check whether the outage is local to one register or store‑wide.

3. Use the manual transaction forms stored at the Customer Service desk.

4. For credit card payments, use the offline card imprinter and record the authorization slip.

5. For cash payments, provide a handwritten receipt.

6. Suspend all returns until the system is restored.

7. Do not provide price overrides without manager approval.

8. Once the system is back online, enter all manual transactions into the POS within two hours.

The IT hotline should be contacted if the outage lasts longer than 15 minutes.
