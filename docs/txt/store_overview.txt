Timber & Tools Co. — Company Overview

Timber & Tools Co. is a regional home‑improvement retailer specializing in lumber, hardware, tools, paint, electrical supplies, plumbing fixtures, and outdoor equipment. The company serves homeowners, DIY hobbyists, and small contractors.

The store mission is to provide reliable products, knowledgeable service, and practical guidance for home‑improvement projects. Timber & Tools Co. emphasizes safety, customer satisfaction, and community involvement.

Each store includes the following departments: Lumber, Hardware, Tools, Paint, Plumbing, Electrical, Garden & Outdoor, and Customer Service.

Store hours are Monday through Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
