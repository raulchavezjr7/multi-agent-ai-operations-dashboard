Timber & Tools Co. — Inventory Management Procedures

Inventory counts are performed weekly by department leads.
All incoming shipments must be checked against the purchase order before stocking shelves.

Damaged items must be logged and placed in the designated return‑to‑vendor area.
Fast‑moving items such as screws, nails, and drill bits should be restocked daily.

If an item is out of stock, employees should offer to place a special order for the customer.
