Timber & Tools Co. — Lumber Product Knowledge

Timber & Tools Co. carries dimensional lumber, plywood, pressure‑treated boards, and specialty hardwoods.

Pressure‑treated lumber is recommended for outdoor projects such as decks and fences.
Plywood thickness ranges from 1/4 inch to 3/4 inch and is used for subflooring, cabinetry, and general construction.

Hardwoods such as oak and maple are used for furniture and interior finishing.

Employees should help customers choose the correct grade and type of lumber based on moisture exposure, load requirements, and finish quality.
