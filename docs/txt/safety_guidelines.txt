Timber & Tools Co. — Safety Guidelines

Employees must follow safety procedures at all times.

- Wear protective gloves when handling lumber or sharp materials.

- Use safety glasses when cutting wood or metal.

- Do not operate power tools without proper training.

- Keep aisles clear of debris and product carts.

- Report spills immediately and block off the area until cleaned.

- Forklift operation is restricted to certified employees only.

- Never leave ladders unattended in customer areas.

In case of injury, notify the manager and complete an incident report.
